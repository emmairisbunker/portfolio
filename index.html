Visual Merchandising & Installation

Window Display Design
& Installation

Art Shop Project 2025

I worked alongside my best friend, Alysse, to design a window display for an art stroll event. The art stroll committee gave us a theme, and we developed a concept, created a sketch, and submitted a formal bid to be considered. Once selected, we sourced props, built a budget, and communicated with the event manager to make sure everything was aligned.

After planning, Alysse and I tackled the full physical installation. We arranged every prop and design element to create a display that was cohesive, polished, and visually impactful to passing foot traffic on the open street.

The Production Edit Agency

The Edit Production Agency is a fictional company that I developed a full twelve-page business proposal for from the ground up. I researched the fashion pre-production market, identified a gap between designers and manufacturers, and built a concept around solving it.

I wrote the brand overview, defined the target customer, mapped out the competitive landscape, and developed a complete marketing and communications strategy including channel selection, brand voice, and pricing structure.
